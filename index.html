Hey ya'll! Welcome! I'm Brittany and I'm a pop culture addict. One of my favorite things to do is combine my love for pop culture with my passion for fashion. As a plus sized girl, I had a hard time finding examples on how to make cosplay work for my body type. This is why I specialize in "casual cosplay" (or specifically, "Disneybounding" when it comes to Disney inspired cosplay), where I can use real life items of clothing that I can find online and in stores to mimic the style of my favorite characters. Need style inspiration for your next theme park adventure? Or do you want to add whimsical touches to your everyday outfits? This (and my Instagram) is the place to be!
